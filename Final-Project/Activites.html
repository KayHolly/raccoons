Activites for this cheesy Festival

Here is a list of activies offered at this year cheese rolling Wake

Wrestling for the belt

Willing participate in a westling match to see who will win the belt

Shin-Kicking

a brutal comppetition in which you face another, kicking each other shin to see who will bend their knee first.

Grining through a horse's collar

a more lighthearted competition. in this activity you will put your head through a horse's collar and make the biggest, goofiest face you can. the prize for this is CAKE.

Maypole

Let your boddy move with the flow with the maypole dance. its simple, grab a rope, choose a direction and dance towards it. as your dancing the sash will begin to cover the pole. Once it is fully coverd marks the end of the dance
